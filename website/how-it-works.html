How Scan & Wear Works

For physical retail stores

1

Generate QR Codes

Upload products to the self-service portal. Generate unique QR codes for each garment ($3 per product).

2

Print & Attach

Print QR tags (we provide print-ready files) and attach to garment tags or store displays.

3

Shopper scans QR with phone camera. Opens a mobile-optimized try-on page instantly — no app needed.

Upload & See Result

Customer uploads a selfie, sees the virtual try-on, and can share with dual watermarks (phone + screen).
