FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How accurate is the virtual try-on?
+
Our AI model (IDM-VTON) scores 9.5/10 on photorealistic accuracy benchmarks. It correctly handles fabric drape, wrinkles, body proportions, and lighting in 3–5 seconds. Customers consistently say it's the most accurate virtual try-on they've seen.
How long does rollout take?
+
Once you sign up and share your garment catalog, we generate your QR codes within a few business days. You print them, attach them to tags or displays, and you're live — no developers, no integration work.
What devices does Scan & Wear work on?
+
Any modern smartphone with a camera and a browser — iOS or Android. Customers point their camera at the QR code, the try-on page opens in the browser, they upload a selfie, and the result appears on screen.
Do customers need to download an app?
+
No app download required. Scan & Wear runs through the phone camera and a mobile web page — customers see the fit instantly without installing anything.
How is pricing calculated?
+
You pay a one-time setup fee to get started, then a small per try-on fee based on usage. There are no monthly subscriptions. Volume discounts are available for brands doing 5,000+ try-ons per month. See our full pricing page for details.
Is customer data kept private and secure?
+
Absolutely. Photos are processed in real-time and not stored on our servers unless the customer explicitly saves their look. We are GDPR-compliant and all data is encrypted in transit and at rest on Google Cloud infrastructure.
